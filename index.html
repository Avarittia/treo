About Us

TREO is an IT startup that was founded with the goal of providing comprehensive and high-quality frontend development and web development services to businesses of all sizes. Our goal is to support companies establish a strong online presence and increase their reach through custom-designed and user-friendly websites. We understand the importance of having a website that not only looks great, but also performs well and delivers results.

Our team of experienced developers is dedicated to creating beautiful and functional websites that drive results for our clients. We are a team with a passion for design and development, who strive to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the industry.
